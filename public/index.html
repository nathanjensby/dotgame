INSTRUCTIONS

  • The game starts when a player touches or clicks the Start button; at that point, the Start button changes to a Pause button, which will pause the game until the button is touched or clicked again.
  • Nic Cages fall at a constant rate. The slider controls the rate at which Nic Cages fall; at the slider's bottom-most position, Nic Cages fall at a speed of 10px per second, and at the slider's top-most position, fall at a speed of 100px per second.
  • A new Nic Cage appears at a random horizontal position at the top of the box every second.
  • Nic Cages vary randomly in size from 10px in diameter to 100px in diameter.
  • A Nic Cage's value is inversely proportional to its size, with the smallest Nic Cages worth 10 points, and the largest Nic Cages worth 1 point.
  • When a player touches or clicks a Nic Cage, the Nic Cage disappears from the box, and the score is increased based on the Nic Cage's value.
